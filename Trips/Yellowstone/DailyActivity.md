# 🌄 Yellowstone & Grand Teton Trip (Part 1)
**Dates Covered:** May 23 – May 26  
**From/To:** Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

---

## 🗓️ Day-by-Day Plan

---

### ✈️ Saturday, May 23 — Arrival
- Fly into Jackson Hole Airport (~9 PM)
- Pick up rental car
- Quick grocery stop (milk, water, snacks)
- Check in: The Lodge at Jackson Hole  
- Relax and sleep early

---

### 🌄 Sunday, May 24 — Jenny Lake + Teton Village
- Early start (~6:30 AM)

**Jenny Lake (Grand Teton National Park):**
- Boat ride across the lake  
- Walk to Hidden Falls  
- Optional: Inspiration Point  

**Afternoon:**
- Lunch in Teton Village  
- Jackson Hole Aerial Tram  
  - Ride to the top  
  - Waffle at Corbet’s Cabin  

**Evening:**
- Return to hotel  
- Relax (pool / hot tub)

---

### 🏔️ Monday, May 25 — Tetons + Jackson Town
- Start (~7:30–8:00 AM)

**Morning (Grand Teton):**
- Schwabacher Landing  
- Snake River Overlook  
- Optional: Taggart Lake short hike  

**Afternoon (Jackson Town):**
- Lunch near Town Square  
- Walk around:
  - Antler arches  
  - Shops + ice cream  

**Evening:**
- Dinner in Jackson  
- Relax at hotel

---

### 🚗 Tuesday, May 26 — Travel to Yellowstone
- Leave Jackson (~8:00 AM)

**Stops:**
- Oxbow Bend (quick scenic stop)  
- Enter Yellowstone via South Entrance  
- West Thumb Geyser Basin (boardwalk walk)  

**Midday:**
- Light lunch (picnic / Grant Village)

**Afternoon:**
- Drive to West Yellowstone  
- Check in: Hibernation Station  

**Evening:**
- Grocery stop  
- Easy dinner  
- Relax at cabin
