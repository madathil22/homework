## Verified Notes

- Yellowstone South Entrance to West Thumb is projected to be open May 8-Oct 31, 2026. This supports the May 26 drive from Jackson into Yellowstone, but weather can still change road status.
- Jackson Hole Aerial Tram is listed for May 16-Oct 4, 2026, 9 AM-5 PM. Your May 24 tram plan fits the published summer window.
- Jenny Lake parking is limited in summer. Keep the early start.
- Jenny Lake shuttle makes Hidden Falls and Inspiration Point much shorter than hiking around the lake.
